FAQs

Goal Symmetry

Q: What is the purpose of this app?

A: The purpose of this app is to use it as a pain free and enjoyable tool for setting and tracking your goals and ambitions.

Q: How does the app work?

A: The first version of the app was designed in such a way that everything is already set up for you. There are divisions: Short Term, Long Term And Dreams. Within each of those divisions, are categories: Financial, Health, Skills, Family etc. Within each of those categories, you can create your goal list accordingly. Once a goal is created, you can set a deadline, update progress or edit it as desired.

Q: Is the app available on iOS and Android?

A: Yes! It's available on both platforms.

Q: What happens to my goal lists if I delete the app or lose my device?

A: Rest assured your goal lists are safe and sound on a secure Firebase database. When you login with your username and password, whatever the device, you can access your goal lists.

Q: Is it free?

A: Yes, the app is free to download but not all features and benefits are available to free users. There's also a free trial for you to test out all the bells and whistles. A lot of planning and work has gone into this app. The creator is also commited to bringing regular updates and additions to make the experience better and better for faithful users.

Q: When is the app going to be ready?

A: The plan is to have the first version of the app ready by May 2021. Possibly sooner.
