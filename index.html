App Development

Josh started getting into iOS development in 2016 shortly after having completed an introductory Computer Science course through Harvard University. Josh then was introduced to Xcode and the two main languages used in iOS development, Objective-C and Swift. It was at that moment; Josh decided that developing iPhone and iPad apps was what he wanted to do. Starting with Objective-C, Josh swiftly moved over to Swift and found it to be the more suitable and broadly utilised choice across the board. After four years, Josh had managed to produce a number of different apps ranging from simple calculation applications to applications incorporating technologies such as machine learning and even augmented reality.

Josh has grown in leaps and bounds, but his development journey doesn't end there. As of July 2020, Josh has invested in time and education to transition over to cross platform app development using Google's Flutter. Now the limitations of just the Apple market are no more. Being able to cater to the Apple as well as the Android market has opened up a lot more opportunities and potential.

The next logic step was to start a business. That's exactly what Josh has done. Keep an eye out for some great productivity apps to hit the market. They'll be produced by Josh and his team at Sagacious Apps Ltd. Do you perhaps have a great app idea and need some help on how to get started? Drop Josh a message.

Web Development

Josh's exposure to web development goes a bit further back than his app development exposure. He began learning the fundamentals of HTML, CSS, JS and SQL in 2013, continuing through 2015 when he studied Computer Science and IT in South Africa. Despite having learnt a lot in those three years of studies, Josh endeavoured to broaden his skill set by learning and pursuing mobile application development. Nevertheless, when 2020 came around, there were a few web development opportunities that arose. So, Josh made the decision to relearn, update some old skills and build upon them by jumping right into learning Bootstrap & experimenting with jQuery.

Josh has regained his confidence in web development and is now more than capable of producing professional and effiicient business level websites for clients. Feel free to browse through his entire freelancer website as well as his business website . They may each be built ontop of Bootstrap templates as a foundation, however all the customisation & interactivity was all him.
